Cut Backs In Services

NYC has seen cuts in services and millions have been affected by these losses. We are striving to restore services to NYCers, especially to those already dealing with housing issues. Please tell your friends about us and reach out to us if you have been affected. Thank you.

Rise In Crime

Crime spikes have been the new pandemic throughout NYC. One issue is the spikes of violent crime seen on trains and to our elderly. Our goal is to continue to ensure our city is safe for everyone.
